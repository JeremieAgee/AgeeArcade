About Agee Arcade

Agee Arcade is designed and built entirely by Jeremie Agee — a solo developer who has always had a deep love for games and coding. That combination is what drives this project: a genuine passion for the craft and a desire to create experiences people can enjoy.

The arcade is dedicated to 3D web-based games — free to play, right in your browser, no downloads required. New games are actively in development and will continue to be added over time.
